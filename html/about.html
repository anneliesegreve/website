Anneliese Greve is a visual artist and spatial researcher based in Berlin and The Hague. After studying at the Berlin University of the Arts, she completed a master's degree in Spatial Design at the Royal Academy of Arts in The Hague. Based on her curiosity for the human and natural environment, her mixed media practice is reminiscent of an archaeological approach and raises questions about our definitions of the natural and the human-made. Her works often evoke associations with artifacts and depict scenarios in which nature becomes fiction and the artificial becomes a new reality.
