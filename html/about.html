I work with organic materials such as ceramics and clay at the interface between drawing, installation, and sculpture. I transform my researches about the relationship between the working human and nature into installations in which fragments of sentences and pictorial details shift the boundaries between time, space, fiction, and reality. I have always felt the urge not to rest on acquired knowledge but to keep on researching, continuously determining new questions, and finding answers to them.
