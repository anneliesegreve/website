I work at the interface between drawing, installation, and sculpture, emphasizing the point of contact between man and civilization, between human order and natural chaos. Fragments of sentences and pictorial details shift the boundaries between time, space, fiction, and reality. I am interested in the human awareness of the vulnerability of nature, prehistoric and primitive cultures and their interaction with nature, their hunting and gathering.
