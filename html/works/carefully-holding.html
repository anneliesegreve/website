Carefully Holding

Carefully Holding, 2021, fine art print, aludibond, glazed ceramic, display case, 58,5 x 41,7 cm x 16,5 cm

What if I entrusted you with my bones? Fragile, near-natural hand bones made of ceramic. Carved into each bone is a fragment of a sentence relating to time or nature that I have collected from various media. The viewer is entrusted to take the bones and read them. Depending on the order of the bones catechized, the reader decides where the story starts and ends.
